# AERIS
Aeris is a digital showcase inspired by the philosophy of Porsche-level engineering and timeless performance, translating precision, restraint, and driving purity into a refined online experience. Built with a front-end–first mindset, the platform emphasizes clarity, motion, and confidence, allowing the vehicles and their engineering to remain the focal point. Every interaction is deliberate—reflecting the balance of performance and elegance expected from a modern performance-luxury marque.

## What it does
- Performance-driven brand presentation
- Communicates Aeris’ design philosophy, motorsport heritage, and engineering mindset with understated confidence and technical clarity.
- Experience-led visual storytelling
- Showcases form, aerodynamics, and craftsmanship through structured layouts, high-contrast black-and-white visuals, cinematic transitions, and a central 3D vehicle presentation.
- Seamless, responsive performance
- A lightweight, high-performance front end optimized for fluid animations, precise interactions, and consistent behavior across all devices—mirroring the responsiveness of a performance vehicle.
- Precision-focused visual language
- A minimal, monochrome design system with sharp typography, disciplined spacing, and subtle motion reflects the exacting standards of performance automotive design.

## Build timeline
- Front-End Engineering, Aura.build - vibe coding with GitHub Copilot.
- Developed with modern front-end technologies to deliver speed, stability, and long-term scalability—prioritizing performance as a core design principle.
- Design System & Visual Hierarchy
- Crafted with a minimal, Porsche-inspired UI system emphasizing proportion, alignment, and clarity over decoration.
- Interaction-First Development
- Focused on scroll-based motion, responsive navigation, and an interactive vehicle customization experience that feels mechanical, precise, and intuitive.
- Performance-Led Art Direction
- Visual decisions guided by concepts of balance, airflow, and control—echoing the engineering logic behind high-performance sports cars.

## Platform vision
Many automotive websites rely on excess—overdesigned visuals or generic layouts. Aeris operates with restraint. The platform balances performance and luxury, letting precision, motion, and structure communicate value. From exploring performance models to customizing a vehicle or understanding engineering philosophy, Aeris delivers a digital experience that feels confident, engineered, and unmistakably performance-focused.
